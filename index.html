‚ö° Quick Start

  1. 1Install Node.js
    Download from nodejs.org (18+) and restart your computer
  2. 2Download & Extract
    Extract the zip file anywhere on your PC
  3. 3Enable RCON
    Edit \astroneer\Astro\Saved\Config\WindowsServer\AstroServerSettings.ini 
    ConsolePort=5000
ConsolePassword=your-password-here
    Restart your Astroneer server
  4. 4Configure Bridge
    Open TakaroConfig.txt in Notepad and edit: 
    IDENTITY_TOKEN=your-server-name
REGISTRATION_TOKEN=get-from-takaro-dashboard
RCON_PASSWORD=same-as-step-3
  5. 5Start the Bridge
    Double-click start.bat
